About Us

Our young dynamic company, working together with partners to supply good quality new and second hand clothes and foot ware for our customers. We welcome every company, big or small from any parts of the world to build reliable partnership. We dedicate ourselves to search nations around our planets and offer Meliota Group Inc. products for communities and companies, where our recycled clothes can start a new life and make good perception for people. If you feel that we can help your organization please feel free to contact us.

Terms

Payments:30% deposit is required to confirm your order, final 70% to be paid before loading. Final balance will vary depending on packing list. Prices are EXW (Ex Works). All sales are final. We accept bank transfer or cash.
Orders:The minimum order quantity is 20 000kg .We do not allow customers to select there own goods in person. You are welcome to come and view the goods. Viewings by appointment only.
Transportation: by request of the buyer we can provide a transport support.
